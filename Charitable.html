BRICKHHAUS CHARITABLE PARTNERS PROGRAM

Like Monsieur Montluc, a 17th century French author, the members of Brickhaus believe that, “Charity well directed should begin at home.” This means being a good neighbor. In this respect, the $15 million* dollar investment Brickhaus is making in the Riversouth community will bring new families to the neighborhood who in turn will support local merchants, restauranteurs, gallery owners, and the cultural constitutions that make Rocky River – Fairview Park region so desirable. In addition, recognizing the financial challenges faced by many of the area’s nonprofit institutions, Brickhaus’ Charitable Partners Programs encourages local philanthropy – any charitable contribution made by a purchaser of a Brickhaus Modern Lifestyle Townhome, up to $1000, to a non-profit entity will be proud matched by Brickhaus.
