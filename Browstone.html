THE RED DOOR CHURCH

Nestled within the exposed shale cliffs of the Rocky River watershed sits one of North America’s most significant architectural statements. Eleven custom-designed private residences cantilever out over the water featuring rooftop kitchens with outdoor fireplaces, living green roofs and state-of-the-art Control 4® home automation technology. On Cleveland’s west side, the Eleven River Luxury Townhomes are located in a completely private enclave just south of Detroit Road, overlooking Rocky River and the Cleveland Metro Parks. The Eleven River Luxury homes hold the Gold Standard for green building and offer the most advanced in sustainable and eco-friendly technology featuring innovations from geothermal heating and cooling to Nichiha eco-conscious exteriors while also offering proximity to the Cleveland Metro Parks, fine dining, and luxurious boutiques.
