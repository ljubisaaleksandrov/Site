LIVESMARTER

Brickhaus is dedicated to providing a sustainable lifestyle. Through the designs and eco-friendly building methods of our homes we have developed communities that meet the needs of the present without compromising the future. The energy efficient features within the homes and the walkability-centered communities fulfill our obligation to be environmentally responsible.

SUSTAINABLE FEATURES

COMMITTED PRACTICES

  • Efficient use of square footage to reduce overall heating and cooling load of your home.
  • Construction Waste Management/Recycling.
  • Efficient framing techniques to minimize waste.
  • Air sealing and Moisture Control. Use highly durable products suited for local environments (i.e. long winters, exposed building sites, etc.).
  • Knowledge and experience in using products and systems, to ensure durability and efficiency.
  • Build low maintenance homes in conjunction with creating a well-designed maintenance program.
